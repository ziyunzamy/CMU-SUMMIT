Sustainability Panel

Edwin Piñero


Ed Pinero works to support programs at company operations and to facilitate relations with key stakeholders in regard to sustainability as well as development and implementation of sustainable programs, concerning with infrastructure, water, energy, and waste. Before, Ed Pinero has served in the public sector as the White House Federal Environmental Executive, White House Council on Environmental Quality, where he focused on developing and implementing policies and practices within the federal government, and the Pennsylvania state government in energy and sustainability.





Jeaneen A. Zappa


Zappa leads the Pittsburgh, PA- based non-profit, Conservation Consultants Inc., which focuses on residential energy efficiency and home performance, especially for low-income households. CCI is driving an effort to convene a statewide working group on the Department of Energy’s Home Energy Score and facilitates the regional ReEnergize Pittsburgh Coalition. Zappa serves on the Board of Directors of the Allegheny Land Trust and Pittsburgh Green Innovators, and she is a former member of the Pittsburgh Technology Council’s Green Advisory Council, the Nine Mile Run Watershed Association Board, Crafton Public Library Board, Hollow Oak Land Trust Board, and the PowerLink Board. Her previous professional roles include serving as the first Sustainability Manager for Allegheny County, Deputy Director of the Green Building Alliance, Director of Strategic Marketing for Descartes Systems Group and Marketing Manager for Vocollect voice recognition technology.





Susan P. Legros


Ms. LeGros has practiced environmental and natural resources law for over 20 years. Her work at CSSD promotes collaborative efforts by industry and its stakeholders called for by the Shale Gas Production Subcommittee of the U.S. Secretary of Energy’s Advisory Board. LeGros has advised chemical, battery and renewable energy companies on regulatory issues and defended them against claims of contamination. Before moving to corporate and private sectors, LeGros served as a section chief at Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) in the mid-1970s.

High Tech Panel

Zhifei Li


As the founder and CEO of Mobvoi, Zhifei Li earned his Ph.D degree in Computer Science at The Johns Hopkins University, becoming an expert in AI robots. The open source machine translation toolkit “Joshua” he developed now becomes a prevailing translation tool in the academia. After graduation, Li joined Google as a Research Scientist and developed an off-line cellphone translation system. After acquiring angel investment from IDG Fund and Sequoia Capital, Li started his own company Mobvoi and aimed for a new generation of voice searching APP, a Google Now in Chinese. The APP is a search tool that can be managed completely with human natural language input. So far, Mobvoi company now possesses an expertise team of engineers from most famous universities, including Stanford, Maryland, Zhejiang University and Fudan University. In June 2015, Mobvoi launched first Chinese voice interaction smartwatch, Ticwatch, which became the leading smartwatch brand in China. Ticwatch features Mobvoi's own Ticwear OS.





Huazhi Hu


Huazhi Hu, Founder and CEO of Ehang, is in charge of the management and product development of the drone company. In 2013, Hu step his foot into developing Artificial Intelligence Drone and started his own company Ehang in 2014. During the years in Ehang, Hu leads his research team to give birth to Ehang Ghost 4-axis multi-rotor drones for individual consumers, and other larger drones for business use. As a master among airplane-models fans, Hu has 15 years of experience in the research on aeromodelling and is the first to build a Museum of Aeromodelling in Beijing, China. Furthermore, Hu has ample of knowledge in the development of software and hardware. He earned a degree in Computer Science at TsingHwa University and designed a camera systems for infants, a Car Computer System, and the first Car Invert Power in China. Hu has also participated in great projects, such as the dispatching systems/ platforms in 2008 Beijing Olympics Game, in Beijing 999 UCC (urgency-care-center) and in Guangdong Province.





Cheng Lv


Founder of Raven Tech, Cheng Lv earned his degree at University of Liverpool. Before Raven Tech, Lv founded timeet in the Junior year, and acquired million-dollar of angel investment to found Raven Tech in Graduate years. Founded in May 2014, Raven Tech is the one of the fifth batch of graduates from Microsoft Ventures Accelerator, and the only startup from Mainland China enrolled in the Y Combinator W15. Its mission is to make our lives easier, more elegant and more efficient with the power of technology and design. Raven Tech focuses in the next generation of human-machine and human-human interaction system, which is called Project Flow. Music Flow, a pure simple music player, is the music feature of Project Flow. It is the way users interact with mobile phones and intelligent terminals in the future.





Zheng Xin


Zheng Xin, CEO, Founder and chairman, earned his degrees in Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics and China Academy of Space Technology (CAST). In 2014, Xin founded ANTVR and developed new product ANTVR KIT, Virtual Reality device. Steve was recognized as Dealmaker of the Year in three times since 2007.
