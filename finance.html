Financial Technology

Panel Description

Over the past few years, digital and technological revolution has definitely changed the landscape in the financial service industry, creating both challenges and opportunities. Featuring the topic of “FinTech”, the 5th CMU Summit finance panel has attracted specialists from leading fintech companies to reveal the mystery of fintech and to disclose world’s new initiatives of financial technology. We aim to better incorporate cutting-edge technologies into traditional finance in the era of innovation. We kindly welcome anyone who is interested in this thriving area to join us and contribute to the the panel discussion with your mind-blowing ideas.

Topics

  • Future of Fintech: Collaboration without boundaries
  • Bridging Finance and Technology: New Opportunities in the Era of Innovative Technology
  • Digital Transformation in Financial Services
  • The trends of Digital Wallet and online payment platform in U.S and China
  • New opportunities with P2P technology
