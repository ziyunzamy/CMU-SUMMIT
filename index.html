Innovation & Entrepreneurship

This year, IE Panel will bring up discussions about the industry trend, investment tendency and commercialization of technology in the leading edge industry, Robotics. The IE panel is dedicated to creating a platform for prestigious investors and entrepreneurs from both China and US to network, share experiences, exchange ideas and moreover to promote bilateral dialogue and cooperation between the two country.

Start-up

As the brand new section of 5th CMU Summit, the Start-up Panel will gather the founders of mature startups, who will introduce their companies and products. During the introduction, the speakers will share with audience invaluable lessons gained from their entrepreneurship experiences. The Start-up Panel aims to build up a communication platform between sophistic and young entrepreneurs for seeking and cultivating the next “Unicorn”.

Sustainability

Energy and Sustainability Panel is one of the five major panels of CMU Summit. This event is featured with keynote speech, panel discussion and Q&A session. We aim to give CMU students insight about the cutting-edge technological developments in energy and sustainability, help students of interest establish better understanding of sustainable energy industries, and become prepared for their professional life. We promise an open space for academic talks, experience sharing, and collision of ideas.

Financial Technology

Over the past few years, digital and technological revolution has definitely changed the landscape in the financial service industry, creating both challenges and opportunities. Featuring the topic of “FinTech”, the 5th CMU Summit finance panel has attracted specialists from leading fintech companies to reveal the mystery of fintech and to disclose world’s new initiatives of financial technology. We aim to better incorporate cutting-edge technologies into traditional finance in the era of innovation. We kindly welcome anyone who is interested in this thriving area to join us and contribute to the the panel discussion with your mind-blowing ideas.
